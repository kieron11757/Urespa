Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

Introduction

Urespa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Name, gender (optional), and quiz responses
  • Content: Scenarios, reflections, comments, and responses you post
  • Partner Connections: Invitation codes and partner relationships you create

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Points, streaks, badges, completed quizzes
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Analytics: App usage patterns (anonymized and aggregated)

Information from Third Parties

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous user IDs for account management
  • Firebase Firestore: Your content and profile data stored securely

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide Services: Enable quizzes, reflections, scenarios, and partner comparisons
  2. Personalize Experience: Customize content and insights based on your profile
  3. Community Features: Share scenarios and reflections (with your consent)
  4. Improve App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  5. Communication: Send notifications about partner connections and updates

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Public Information

  • Scenarios: Scenarios you post are visible to all users (unless posted anonymously)
  • Reflections: Reflections you share are visible to the community (unless shared anonymously)
  • Quiz Insights: Your quiz answers contribute to aggregated, anonymous statistics

Anonymous Data

  • Quiz answer statistics are aggregated and anonymized
  • Gender-based insights are optional and anonymous
  • No personally identifiable information is included in statistics

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: We use Firebase for authentication and data storage
  • Firebase Privacy: Firebase complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Track you across other apps or websites

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted
  • Authentication: Anonymous authentication for secure access
  • Access Controls: Firestore security rules limit data access
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update security measures

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

  • View your profile information in the app
  • Update your name and gender at any time
  • Review your quiz results and reflections

Data Deletion

  • Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
  • Contact us for assistance with data deletion

Privacy Controls

  • Post scenarios and reflections anonymously
  • Choose whether to share your gender for insights
  • Control partner connections and invitations

User-Generated Content

Important Notice: All content shared on Urespa (scenarios, responses, comments, replies) represents the individual views and opinions of users, NOT the views or opinions of the app maker, developers, or Urespa team. The app maker is not responsible for user-generated content.

Children's Privacy

Urespa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are using Urespa from outside the United States, please note that your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
