Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

When you first open Urespa, you'll be guided through the account creation process. You can sign in anonymously using Firebase Authentication, which doesn't require an email address.

How do I connect with my partner?

Go to the Partner section in the app and generate an invitation code. Share this code with your partner, and they can enter it to connect with you. Once connected, you can compare quiz results and insights.

Can I post anonymously?

Yes! When posting scenarios or reflections, you have the option to post anonymously. Your content will be visible to the community, but your name won't be displayed.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data through the app settings. Navigate to your profile, then to settings, and select "Delete Account". This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What should I do if I see inappropriate content?

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, please use the reporting feature in the app. Tap the flag icon on any post to report it. Our moderation team will review reported content promptly.

How are quiz insights calculated?

Quiz insights are based on aggregated, anonymized responses from all users. Your individual answers contribute to these statistics, but your personal information is never included in the insights shown to others.

Can I change my profile information?

Yes, you can update your name and gender preferences at any time through your profile settings in the app.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use Firebase for secure data storage and authentication. All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted, and we follow industry best practices for data security.

Common Issues & Solutions

App won't load or crashes

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device

Can't connect with partner

  • Verify that the invitation code is entered correctly
  • Make sure both devices have internet connectivity
  • Try generating a new invitation code
  • Check that both users have completed the initial setup

Content not appearing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down to refresh the content
  • Wait a few moments and try again
  • If the issue persists, try logging out and back in

Getting Started

New to Urespa? Here's how to get the most out of the app:

  1. Complete your profile: Add your name and preferences to personalize your experience
  2. Take quizzes: Explore the available quizzes to learn more about yourself
  3. Connect with your partner: Share an invitation code to enable partner comparison features
  4. Engage with the community: Post scenarios, share reflections, and learn from others
  5. Track your progress: Check your points, streaks, and badges in your profile
